YNAB Going Dutch


Converts your Dutch bank statements from their CSV to YNAB CSV format

This tool is created to automatically detect and convert your CSV files to a format that is used by "You Need A Budget" (YNAB).

Usage is simple: Download the CSV file of your supported bank (this csv can contain information about multiple accounts), throw it in the converter, the converter will automatically prompt a download with the converted file.
Find the source on Github.
